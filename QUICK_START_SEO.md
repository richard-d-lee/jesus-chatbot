### Step 4: Submit to Google (10 minutes)

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter your URL
4. Verify ownership (meta tag already in HTML - no action needed!)
5. Go to "Sitemaps" in left menu
6. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
7. Click "Submit"
8. Go to "URL Inspection"
9. Enter your homepage URL
10. Click "Request Indexing"

### Step 5: Test Everything (5 minutes)

**Rich Results Test:**
1. Go to https://search.google.com/test/rich-results
2. Enter your URL
3. Verify you see: WebApplication, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList

**Mobile-Friendly Test:**
1. Go to https://search.google.com/test/mobile-friendly
2. Enter your URL
3. Verify it passes

**PageSpeed Insights:**
1. Go to https://pagespeed.web.dev/
2. Enter your URL
3. Check scores (aim for 80+ on mobile)
